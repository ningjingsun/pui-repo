Medical Imaging Analysis


Medical Imaging Analysis is a technique of imaging the interior of a body part for clinical analysis and medical intervention, as well as visual representation of the function of some organs or tissues. This feature could identify potential disease and abnormalities of users, and help medical practitioners to create more accurate diagnosis and prognosis.


Industry: Healthcare
Examples: Mammogram screenings/analysis, MRI brain analysis, fractures/musculoskeletal injuries, Cardiac imaging
